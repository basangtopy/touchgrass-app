## The Philosophy: Why TouchGrass Works

### The Psychology of Commitment

Let's have an honest conversation. You _know_ what you should do‚Äîexercise more, read more, scroll less, drink more water. But knowing isn't doing. There's always a gap between intention and action.

TouchGrass bridges that gap using a powerful psychological principle called **Loss Aversion**. Studies show we're about twice as motivated to avoid losing something as we are to gain something of equal value. When your actual money is on the line, suddenly that morning run feels a lot more achievable.

But we're not here to punish you. We're here to help you become the person you've always wanted to be.

## When Things Don't Go as Planned

Hey, it happens. Life gets in the way sometimes. Here's what to expect if you don't verify in time.

### The Penalty Kicks In

Whatever penalty type you chose during setup now executes. But remember:

Depending on your penalty type:

### It's a Learning Experience

Even a failed challenge teaches you something. Maybe you set the bar too high, chose the wrong timeframe, or life threw you a curveball. Adjust and try again. Every setback is a setup for a comeback.

When you create a challenge, you choose what happens if you don't follow through. Choose wisely‚Äîthis is your accountability mechanism.

### üíö Give to Health Charity

Your stake is donated to public health causes. Your failure becomes someone else's gain. It's the "feel-good" penalty.

**Best for:** People who want a safety net of meaning. If you fail, at least you did some good.

### üõ†Ô∏è Support the Dev Team

Your stake goes to the TouchGrass treasury to fund ongoing development and improvements.

**Best for:** Those who believe in the project and want to contribute either way.

### üîê Timeout Mode (Lock)

No money is lost, but your funds are frozen for **5x your original challenge duration**. Failed a 24-hour challenge? Your funds are locked for 5 days.

**Best for:** Those who want accountability without financial loss. Time is money, after all.

### üî• Burn It All

Your stake is sent to a dead address and destroyed forever. Nobody gets it. Maximum stakes, maximum motivation.

**Best for:** The hardcore. Those who need the ultimate "there's no way I'm not doing this" motivation.

### Adjustable Penalty Percentage

For Charity and Dev penalties, you can adjust what percentage of your stake is at risk (20-100%). The rest comes back to you even if you fail.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### "Is my money actually safe?"

**Yes.** TouchGrass is non-custodial. Your funds sit in a smart contract on the blockchain, not in someone's bank account. The code dictates exactly when and how funds can move. Neither developers nor anyone else can touch your stake‚Äîonly you (after meeting the conditions).

### "What if my challenge disappears from my dashboard?"

Sit tight for about 30 seconds. TouchGrass has an Auto-Recovery system that scans the blockchain for your challenges and restores them automatically. Your funds are never lost.

### "Can I submit unlimited verification attempts?"

Yes! Submit as many times as you need before your deadline. Just make sure each photo clearly shows your accomplishment.

### "Why does it say 'Recovered Challenge' on my dashboard?"

That means we restored a challenge from the blockchain, but we couldn't recover its original title. Just tap the pencil icon to rename it.

### "I verified successfully but can't withdraw‚Äîwhat's wrong?"

You probably need a tiny bit of ETH for gas fees. Make sure your wallet has some ETH (even $0.10 is usually enough on Base).

### "Can I cancel a challenge after I start it?"

No. Once you lock your stake, you're committed. That's the whole point! The only way out is to complete the challenge or wait for the penalty.
